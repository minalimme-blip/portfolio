[ 01 ] About me

A product marketer with a teacher's clarity.

Product marketing for technical capital equipment. I turn complex hardware into clear stories distributors can win with — and the always-on enablement layer underneath.

"Teacher in South Korea 👉 Product Marketer in Finland. Breaking complex ideas down so people truly understand — that's what I've always done, just in different rooms."

After five years teaching in Korea, I moved to Finland, earned my Master's from Tampere University, and made the leap into B2B marketing. At Dinolift Oy I own positioning, launch content, and sales enablement for technical capital equipment — partnering daily with Product Management, R&D, and regional sales across a distributor network in 10+ countries.

On the side, I run a YouTube channel in the global top 2.5% of creators — 19K+ subscribers (0 → 10K in 10 months, 1M+ views), 140+ videos translating dense topics into something audiences actually finish. Same instinct, different audience: make complex things land.

AI-Powered Sales Lead Bot — HubSpot Conversational AI

AI / Marketing Ops HubSpot Lead Routing

Problem

Inbound sales inquiries from 10+ distributor markets were landing in a generic inbox — slow routing, missed handoffs, and a lead-cycle bottleneck for both end-buyers and partner distributors.

Execution

  • Deployed an AI-powered conversational bot on the corporate website using HubSpot's conversational AI layer
  • Trained the bot on product knowledge, application use-cases, and distributor coverage logic
  • Built routing rules to connect inquiries instantly to the right distributor or to the internal sales team
  • Integrated with HubSpot lifecycle stages so every bot conversation became a tracked, scored lead

Result

  • 50+ inquiries/month resolved or routed automatically
  • Reduced lead-cycle time — buyers reach the right contact without waiting for manual triage
  • Distributor partners get qualified leads in their region without marketing-ops overhead
  • Established a repeatable AI-ops pattern for future B2B automation
